Digital Identity is …

  • the identifiable data of a person, organization, nation, application or device

  • the unique credentials used to authenticate a person, organization, application, or device in an online environment, and encompasses

  • the technical infrastructure required to create, safely store, and responsibly utilize both the data and the credentials

Impact

Individual

Digital identity attacks at the individual level can lead to identity theft and personal financial loss.

Community

Within communities, these breaches can disrupt local services and erode trust.

Nation

On a national scale, compromised digital identities can weaken critical infrastructure and public confidence.

Global

Globally, such attacks threaten international markets and challenge worldwide security.
